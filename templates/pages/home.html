About Me

My name is Carlos and I live in the city of Hurlingham — Rock capital of the world!
I have a Bachelor's degree in Sociology from the University of Buenos Aires and extensive experience in data engineering and analysis, which helps me connect the dots between technical aspects and real user needs.

After a successful transition from Data Analyst to Data Engineer, I am now focusing on my passion for Software Development. I have extensive experience with Python and the "Modern Data Stack".

I'm also a strong believer in community and contribute to open-source projects, such as the Spanish translation of Python's documentation. I also co-organize local events like PyDay Hurlingham.

If you're interested in the intersection of data, technology, and society, let's connect!
